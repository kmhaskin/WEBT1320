About

We are a punk rock band hailing from the great city of Chattanooga, Tennessee. Our music is a blend of fast-paced guitar riffs, driving basslines, and pounding drum beats that will make you want to mosh all night long. Our lyrics tackle topics such as social justice, individualism, and living life without regrets. We've been playing shows all across the state and are known for our high-energy performances that leave audiences wanting more. Join us as we continue to rock out and spread our message of empowerment and rebellion!
