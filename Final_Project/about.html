A breif history on Abstract art. The origins of abstract art can be traced back to the work of artists such as Wassily Kandinsky, who sought to create works of art that were more spiritual and expressive than their representational counterparts. Abstract art was born at the beginning of the 20th century. At this time the artistic landscape was predominantly made up of fauvism, cubism and figurative expressionism. This type of art is marked by its freedom of color, shapes and of course: it's subject. Here you can learn the history and tools for creating abstract art.We also provide tutorials so you can learn how to create abstract art along with a gallery to get inspired by.Explore and create!
